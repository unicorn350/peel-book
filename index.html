PEEL BOOK

Peel, use, reduce

Notebook made of paper out of fruit and vegeteables peels. With this, we would reduce the excessive cut of trees.

Procedure

First, you need to wash the peels and let them soak for about 15 min in water. Then. you blend them with a bit of water to break its fibers and create a mass. After this, you strain the dough to remove excess of water. then, you shape the mass into the shape you want your paper to be. Finally, it is important to let it dry naturally for as long as you need.
